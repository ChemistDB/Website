Outreach

Judge for the Scholastic Writing Awards to raise funds in support of the MVWP at

Demonstration Leader for Halloween Chemistry outreach activity for a local girl

Group Leader for the Summer Teachers Program funded by the Arizona Science

Science Fair Mentor and Judge at Hidden Hills Elementary School, encouraging

Mentor for undergraduate visiting students through the NSF Model Institutions for

Science Mentor for the St. John-Bosco Middle School science fair, helping 7th and

Student Representative to the ASU Department of Chemistry Undergraduate

    Programs Committee. I organized the participation of the Department of

    (2005-2006)


Vice President of Community Outreach for the Student Affiliates of the American

    Chemical Society. I developed, organized, and conducted chemistry

    demonstrations in schools throughout the Phoenix area. (2004-2006)


Demonstration Leader in the “I’m College Bound” program, an outreach to

    elementary and middle school students to encourage them to consider college

    careers in the sciences.  (2004-2006)
